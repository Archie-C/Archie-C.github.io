rules

House rules

chrome · 1 px axes in --fg-2, 1 px gridlines in --rule
series · 1.5 px stroke, round caps + joins
type · ticks & labels in JetBrains Mono · captions in Source Serif
scale · sentence-case axis labels, eyebrow above each chart
colour · one accent + neutrals · never recolour every series
chartjunk · no 3-d, no shadows, no rounded bar caps, no gradients
annotation · pin facts to the mark with a 0.75 px hairline · never with arrows
density · ≥ 24 px tick spacing · ≤ 4 categorical series per chart
captions · always · fig N label + a sentence in serif

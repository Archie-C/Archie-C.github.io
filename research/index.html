research

Machine learning for clinical recommender systems.

I work on recommender systems in healthcare, especially how machine learning can make better use of electronic health records to support clinical decision-making. My current focus is reproducibility: what survives when published drug recommendation models are rebuilt carefully, tested consistently, and evaluated across patient cohorts.
